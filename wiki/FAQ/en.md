# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**What is Rinari?**
Rinari is a private osu! server built on the Titanic framework. It's currently 
in beta, so expect some rough edges!

**Is Rinari free?**
Yes, completely free.

**What clients are allowed?**
Most clients and modifications are allowed. The only ones explicitly banned are 
Freedom and Maple, as they allow fake replay submission which corrupts server data.

## Accounts

**Can I have multiple accounts?**
No. One account per person, always. The only exception is approved bot accounts.
Contact staff first if you need one.

**I forgot my password / lost access to my email. What do I do?**
Open a ticket in the [Rinari Discord](https://discord.gg/fSmtpH3HSK) or email 
[support@rinarii.de](mailto:support@rinarii.de).

**Can I change my username?**
Contact a staff member about this.

## Gameplay

**Why can't I submit scores?**
Make sure you're properly connected to Rinari's servers. If the problem persists,
open a ticket in the [Rinari Discord](https://discord.gg/fSmtpH3HSK).

**Are cheats allowed?**
Mostly yes, with the exception of Freedom and Maple. See the [Rules](/wiki/en/Rules).

**How do I get my map ranked?**
Contact a [Beatmap Nominator](/wiki/en/People/BN) to have your map reviewed.
