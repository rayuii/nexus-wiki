# Getting Started
Welcome to Rinari! Here's how to get connected.

## Requirements
- A Rinari account (register at [rinarii.de](https://rinarii.de))
- Either the Rinari client or a normal osu! install

## Option 1 — Rinari Client (Recommended)
The easiest way to play on Rinari.

1. Go to [rinarii.de](https://rinarii.de) and click **Download**
2. Install the client
3. Log in with your Rinari credentials and play!

## Option 2 — Standard osu! Client
Already have osu! installed? You can connect directly without downloading anything extra.

1. Find your osu! shortcut or executable
2. Add `-devserver rinarii.de` to the launch arguments
   - **Windows:** Right click your osu! shortcut → Properties → add `-devserver rinarii.de` to the end of the Target field
3. Launch osu! and log in with your Rinari credentials

## Registering
Don't have an account yet?
1. Go to [rinarii.de](https://rinarii.de)
2. Click **Register**
3. Fill in your details and you're good to go!
