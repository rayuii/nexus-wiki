# Rules

Welcome to Nexus! Before you get started, please read through these rules for a more safe and pleasant experience.

## Community Rules

1. **Each user may only have *one* account ever!**
    The only exception to this rule is making bot accounts or other projects, but you must first let staff know so we can approve it. If you cannot access your email or you have forgotten your password, please open a support ticket in the [Nexus Discord](https://discord.gg/fSmtpH3HSK) server or contact the [support email](mailto:support@osu-nexus.ovh).

2. **Play fair.**
    Using any third-party tools that give you an unfair advantage over other players will result in a restriction. This includes programs like macros, cheat engine, aim-assist programs, timewarp, and so on. Additionally, exploiting bugs or unintended behavior in a client is strictly prohibited. If you see suspicious activity from a user, please open a ticket in the [Nexus Discord](https://discord.gg/fSmtpH3HSK) server or contact the [support email](mailto:support@osu-nexus.ovh).

3. **Be kind to people.**
    Any harassment, hate speech, or antagonism has no place here on Nexus. This is a server about clicking circles, catching fruit, bashing drums, and breaking keyboards — not being rude to each other.

4. **No NSFW/18+ content.**
    Any content that contains drug use or topics of a sexual nature are not welcome here.

5. **Malicious impersonation of another person is prohibited.**
    Any attempt to pose as someone else with the intent to deceive or harm will result in an immediate restriction.

6. **Modified osu! clients** *are* permitted on Nexus, but they must receive approval from a server administrator before use. Any attempt to circumvent this requirement will result in a restriction.

7. **Intentional server abuse.**
    Deliberate attempts to overload, DDoS, or otherwise disrupt Nexus's servers are strictly prohibited and will lead to an account restriction.

8. **Unsolicited advertisements** are prohibited on Nexus. Any promotional content must be pre-approved by a staff member before posting. Breaking this rule may result in your account being silenced.

9. **Minimum age requirement.**
    All users must be at least 13 years old to use Nexus. Accounts found to be under this age will be suspended until proof of eligibility is provided.

10. **Use common sense.**
    The staff team has explicit discretion to apply their judgment as they see fit.

## In-game Chat Rules

1. **Be respectful and mindful of other users in the chat.**
    Avoid flooding or spamming. Taking up half the screen by yourself is annoying, so please avoid it whenever possible.

2. **If someone gets silenced or restricted, leave them be.**
    Their case is between them and the administrators.

3. **Do not continue to message or harass users after being blocked.**
    If a user blocks you, it means they no longer wish to communicate. Attempting to bypass the block — especially to harass, disturb, troll, or provoke them — is a serious violation. If someone is harassing you, please open a support ticket in the [Nexus Discord](https://discord.gg/fSmtpH3HSK) or contact the [support email](mailto:support@osu-nexus.ovh).

## Forum-wide Rules

1. **Spamming multiple posts within a single thread is strictly prohibited.**
    Use the edit button to add to your post when needed. Excessive posting disrupts discussions and may lead to post removal or a punishment.

2. **Do not necro-post in General Discussion or Off-Topic threads older than 3 months.**
    If the topic is still relevant, consider starting a new thread instead. Repeated necro-posting may result in a warning or silence.

## Reporting / Support

There are 2 ways to reach our staff team:

- Opening a ticket in the [Nexus Discord](https://discord.gg/fSmtpH3HSK).
- Writing an email to [support@osu-nexus.ovh](mailto:support@osu-nexus.ovh).
